Welcome to George's Place

George's Place Bar and Grill is your top spot for food, drinks, fun, and more. We've been your neighborhood bar since 2005 and still going strong. George's Place is located on 159th Street and Kedie Avenue in Markham Illinois. Since we opened in 2005 George's has become everyone's favorite local bar serving the best selection of alcoholic beverages, award winning food like our famous steak sandwich and offering the best live music, weekend DJs, and frequent special events and parties.
